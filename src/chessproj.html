Chessbot Duel

Welcome to Chessbot Duel! This innovative web application offers a dynamic chess-playing experience, allowing users to engage in games against either a fellow player or a sophisticated chess AI. What sets Chessbot Duel apart is its unique feature that enables users to upload their own AI models for a truly customizable chess experience.

AI Sample Included: Jumpstart your journey with 'Simon', our sample AI player, designed to offer a challenging yet enjoyable game.

Get ready to experience chess like never before with Chessbot Duel, where strategy meets technology.

Why Chessbot Duel? My fascination with creating an AI capable of mastering a complex game like chess was the seed for this project. Realizing there were no platforms available for testing and pitting chess AI against each other, I saw an opportunity. Chessbot Duel is more than just a game; it's a platform for hobbyists and enthusiasts alike to test their AI creations, challenge friends' bots, and enjoy a unique and engaging pastime.
