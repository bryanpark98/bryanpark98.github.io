Discord Librarian ChatBot

A Document-Learning Chatbot for Discord Servers

Overview: Discord Librarian revolutionizes the way users interact with documents on Discord. This innovative chatbot is designed to enable users to upload various documents, from which it learns and assimilates information. Once a document is uploaded, Discord Librarian becomes capable of not only answering specific questions related to the content of the document but also providing concise summaries.

Functionality: Upon integration into a Discord server, Discord Librarian actively engages with users, responding to their queries about the uploaded documents. It's like having a personal librarian at your fingertips, ready to offer insights and summaries of the textual material you provide.

User Experience: This chatbot enhances the Discord experience by making information access and document management both seamless and interactive. Users on the server can simply ask questions and get real-time responses, making it an invaluable tool for educational, professional, or leisure purposes.

Why Discord Librarian? The inspiration behind this project stems from the belief that effective customer support is crucial for the success of small websites. In today's digital age, having a personalized AI that can learn and provide information about your website's content is not just a luxury, but a necessity. Hosting this service on Discord offers an intriguing advantage, as it leverages a platform familiar to many, creating a comfortable and accessible environment for users to interact and learn.
