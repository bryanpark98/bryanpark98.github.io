Therabot

A Reflective AI for Personal Journaling

Overview: Therabot is an AI tool that aids the personal journaling experience. It intelligently summarizes weekly journal entries from Notion, providing users with a thoughtful, encouraging, and motivational weekly recap. This unique approach to journaling helps individuals reflect on their week in a comprehensive and uplifting way.

Functionality: By analyzing weekly journal entries, Therabot generates insightful summaries, focusing on the positive aspects and achievements of the week. Its AI is designed to be thoughtful and sensitive, ensuring that users feel good about their week, regardless of the challenges faced.

User Experience: Therabot offers a new dimension to journaling, enhancing the reflective process. Its summaries serve as a source of motivation and encouragement, helping users to see the progress and positives in their daily lives. It also uses a lot of emojis which is fun!

Why Therabot? I was inspired by the therapeutic potential of reflective journaling, and Therabot aims to offer users a unique way to process their experiences.
